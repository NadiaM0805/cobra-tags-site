How it works

Quick Start

  1. Unbox your Cobra Tag and remove the battery tab.
  2. Choose platform:
    • iPhone: Find My → Items → + → Other Item → follow prompts.
    • Android: Find My Device → Items → Add → follow prompts.
  3. Name your tag (Keys, Bike, Luggage, etc.).
  4. Attach via keyring or stick-on card.

Full instructions are printed in the box and shown in the video.
